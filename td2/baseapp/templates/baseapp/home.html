This is site is currently under development. This means it is not safe for people or their furpals/lizardkin/insectbros and probably wont work AT ALL!

It is being developed for the following purposes:

  • To teach me django, python, bootstrap etc
  • To test out some ideas I had about whether or not the Thunderdome concept, currently running on the Something Awful forums, could be replicated/automated on another platform in the event something happened to those forums (this is, admittedly, less of a risk currently than it used to be, but who knows what the future holds?).
  • To see how easy it is these days to make something with relatively modern tools and make it as accessible and secure as possible
  • To make me incredibly rich by stealing other people's ideas and monetising them, as is the fashion (stretch goal).

How does it work?

  1. Someone creates a contest
  2. >While that contest is open, users can enter stories into it
  3. Currently there is no scheduling (because this site is on a free hosting account), so the contest can be closed by a button on the Current Contests page
  4. All entrants are then assigned three stories to critique. Users can see stories they are in line to judge in Judgemode
  5. User/Judges critiques aren't final until they have submitted with the 'Final' checkbox xhecked, so they can take notes as they read each sotry
  6. Once all the critiques are in (current state viewable in Current Contests another botton on that page closes the competition and shows the result. Again, this would normally be automated by I am incredibibly cheap.
